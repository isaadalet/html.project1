Our Story

One of the most stunning pub interiors in the city of London now given a new lease of life,
paired with honest pub grub and fantastic, Hertfordshire brewed beers.
Welcome to the Old Bank of England!
Our pub resides in the old Law Court’s branch of the previous Bank of England which operated from 1888 to 1975,
although you may not be able to get a loan with us you are bound to receive attentive service,
and a unique experience in the country’s capital.
Take a trip (not very far) on our vintage Routemaster double decker bus in our suntrap garden; ideal for an event,
occasion or just a great pic for Instagram!
